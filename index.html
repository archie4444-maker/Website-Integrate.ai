FAQ

Common Questions.

The questions we get asked most — answered plainly, without the jargon.

What happens to our data?
Data protection is the question we get asked most. We understand exactly what happens with your data inside every tool we recommend. For businesses handling sensitive information, we can implement closed-loop agentic workflows that keep everything local — your data never leaves your infrastructure.
Will this replace jobs in our business?
No. We do not replace jobs — we make them better. Every job is a collection of tasks. Some are genuinely interesting and valuable; others are admin, repetition, and box-ticking that nobody enjoys. We automate the second kind so your team can spend more time on the first. AI levels up the job, it does not delete it.
What are the risks?
AI tools are relatively new and we will not pretend otherwise. We give full transparency on the risk profile of every solution we propose and tailor what we build to your risk tolerance. Most tools today allow you to strictly limit access so you remain in complete control at all times.
How does pricing work?
The initial consultation is always free. We come in, understand your business, and assess the opportunity honestly. If we can help, we agree a fee tied directly to the value we add. If we cannot help, we will tell you.
Do we need technical staff to maintain it?
No. That is the whole point. We build integrations designed to be used and maintained by non-technical people. We train your team, document everything, and stay available for questions. You should not need a developer to keep the lights on.
Which tools do you work with?
We work with the best-in-class tools available today — Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, NotebookLM, n8n, and more. We benchmark constantly and we are vendor-neutral. We recommend what actually works, not what we have a commercial arrangement with.
